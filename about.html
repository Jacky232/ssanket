Hailing from a reputed Gujarati Business Family. Ssanket has been practicing Tarot, Numerology and lots of energy work since long. Ssanket had a family business of Custom clearing & Forwarding but his heart was into occult so on 31st March 2015 he decided to quit his family business and give his full time into the occult business.

He believes everything is energy and when the time comes the universe makes sure you meet the right person who can be a guide and help you sail through your life easily.

Everything happens at the right time and at the right place. He is of the belief that each one is blessed with abundance and harmonizing the effects of one’s destiny & can open vistas and channels of growth and prosperity. He is of the firm belief that has the powers to restore immense positivity and marvel stupendous results.

His forte includes Tarot Card Reading & Teaching with Numerology Solutions in Pythagorean & Chaldean Systems of Numerology with energy work like Chakra Healing, Pendulum Dowsing, Counselling and lots more…..
